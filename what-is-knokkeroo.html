What is knokkeroo?

knokkeroo is a simple daily check-in app: tap one button to tell the people who care about you that you're okay.

The daily "Knok"

Once a day, tap Knok. That's it — the people watching over you (your approved contacts) instantly see that you're fine.

Watching over each other

You and someone you trust — a family member, a neighbour, a friend — both agree to watch each other. Nobody is added without approval on both sides.

If someone doesn't check in within the time window they chose, the people watching them are gently alerted so they know to reach out.

Quiet hours

Everyone sets their own "quiet hours" (like overnight) so a normal night's sleep is never mistaken for a missed check-in.

Free or Unlimited — your choice

knokkeroo is free to use. Here's the difference between the two plans:

Free

Watch over up to 4 people, and be watched by as many as you like. No time limit, no trial — just free.

Unlimited — $4.99/month*

Watch over as many people as you want. Cancel any time from your phone's account settings.

*Price shown in USD; your store shows the exact price in your local currency.

One important note

knokkeroo is a simple way for people who care about each other to stay in touch. It is not a medical alert system or an emergency service, and it does not contact emergency services on your behalf.
