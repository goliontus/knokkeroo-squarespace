About Odderon Phi

Odderon Phi is a small South African technology company building practical, human-focused software.

The company was created as a home for careful technical work: software that solves real problems, respects the people using it, and avoids unnecessary complexity. Odderon Phi is interested in tools that are useful, understandable, and built with discipline, not technology for its own sake.

Our first product, knokkeroo, is a simple check-in companion designed to help people stay lightly connected without turning care into surveillance. It focuses on reassurance, routine, and gentle visibility: a quick knok, a simple status, and a calmer way to notice when someone may need attention.

knokkeroo is intentionally modest in what it claims to do. It is not designed to replace emergency services, family judgement, personal responsibility, or direct human contact. Instead, it supports everyday check-ins with a tool that is simple enough to use regularly and respectful enough not to become intrusive.

Odderon Phi builds carefully, tests thoroughly, and keeps its products deliberately focused. The aim is to create software that reduces friction, provides clarity, and helps people stay connected in ordinary life: calmly, honestly, and without unnecessary noise.
