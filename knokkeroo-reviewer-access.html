1. What the app does

knokkeroo is a simple daily check-in app. A user taps Knok to let the people who care about them know they are okay. Contacts watch over each other through mutual, both-approved connections and are alerted if someone is overdue.

It is a personal communication tool — not a medical or emergency service. Please see the in-app disclaimer and our Terms for the full emergency disclaimer.

2. Demo login — no SMS is sent

Use the reserved demo account below. These accounts do not send any real WhatsApp/SMS. The verification code is fixed, so no message is needed.

  1. On the login screen, open the country selector and choose United States (+1).
  2. Enter phone number 202 555 0100, then tap Send verification code.
  3. Enter code 148392, then tap Verify code.

This signs you in as the primary demo reviewer. All demo accounts start on the FREE plan, so you can review the free tier and the subscription paywall: the first four contacts are fully visible and any further contacts (and the "Add contact" tile) appear as anonymous "locked" tiles that open the paywall.

To see the full, unlocked experience, tap "Subscribe" on the paywall. For demo/review accounts this is simulated — it unlocks every contact immediately with no real transaction, no charge, and no Google Play purchase sheet. Simply log out and back in to return the account to the free view and review the paywall again.

No action in the app can charge a demo account. "Subscribe" and "Restore purchases" are simulated for demo accounts; "Terms of Use", "Not now", and the contact tiles never involve payment. (For real, non-demo accounts the Subscribe button opens Google Play's own purchase sheet, which must be explicitly confirmed and can be cancelled.)

4. Suggested test steps

  1. Contacts screen: after login you see a grid of tiles — green for recently checked in, amber for due, red for overdue, and some with a moon for quiet hours. Three tiles appear per row.
  2. Knok: tap the Knok tile. Your Me tile turns green and updates the time.
  3. Add a contact: tap Add Contact, then enter a name and a number. For demo accounts, no real message is sent.
  4. Respond to a request: tap a purple Approve tile to accept or decline a pending request.
  5. Timezones & quiet hours: the demo contacts are pinned to real timezones across the United States (Pacific, Mountain, Central, Eastern, Alaska) and Europe (London, Berlin, Athens). Each contact's time and quiet-hours moon reflect their own timezone, so they will not match your device's time. That is intentional, to show the feature. A normal, non-demo account instead follows the device's timezone.
  6. Settings: tap Me to change your name, knok interval, and quiet hours.
  7. Account deletion: open Me → Delete account. Demo accounts are protected and show a notice. Real accounts are permanently deleted. See our Account Deletion policy.

5. Notes for reviewers

  • No real SMS/WhatsApp is sent for demo accounts. Logging in and inviting demo contacts triggers no messages.
  • Demo data is reset by us between reviews, so the demo accounts always start in a known state.
  • Demo contacts keep fixed US/European timezones. We never overwrite them. Their displayed times differ from yours on purpose so that timezone and quiet-hours behaviour can be reviewed.
  • The app targets adults and is not directed at children.
  • Subscriptions & billing: the free tier fully shows a user's first four contacts; a paid subscription (sold and billed entirely by Google Play — we never see or store card details) shows all of them. All demo accounts start on the FREE plan. Tapping Subscribe on a demo account is simulated — it unlocks everything with no charge and no Google Play purchase sheet; log out and back in to reset to the free view. For real (non-demo) users, Subscribe opens Google Play's own cancellable purchase sheet. No purchase is required to review the app, and nothing a reviewer taps can charge them.
