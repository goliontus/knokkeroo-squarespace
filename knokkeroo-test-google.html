knokkeroo for Android

Testable now, as a tester

The public Play Store listing is still being reviewed by Google, but you can install and try knokkeroo today as a registered tester. It's the same app, same account — nothing extra to keep track of.

1. Join the tester group: groups.google.com/g/knokkeroo-testers

2. Opt in to testing: play.google.com/apps/testing/com.goliontus.knokker

3. Install from the Play Store as normal.

Use the same Google account for steps 1 and 2 — it can take an hour or two to take effect. Once knokkeroo is publicly live, no tester group will be needed — anyone will be able to just search "knokkeroo".
